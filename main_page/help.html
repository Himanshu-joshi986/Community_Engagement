Help & Support

Welcome to the Help Center for Amchi Shala. We're here to assist you with anything you need to ensure the best learning experience for your child or student.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q: Who can use Amchi Shala?
    A: Amchi Shala is designed for children with mental and developmental challenges, but any child who enjoys interactive learning is welcome.
  • Q: Is Amchi Shala free?
    A: Yes! Core content is free. Additional materials are available with low-cost membership.
  • Q: Do I need to sign in?
    A: You can explore most resources without signing in, but a login is needed to access personalized content and track progress.
