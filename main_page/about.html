About Amchi Shala

Amchi Shala is a joyful learning platform created especially for children with mental and developmental challenges. Our mission is to make education accessible, engaging, and inclusive—because every child deserves the chance to learn at their own pace, in their own way.

Designed by educators and supported by a caring community, Amchi Shala uses interactive tools like colorful games, stories, music, and basic math to help children build skills while having fun. Everything is created with simplicity and ease of use in mind—no pressure, just progress.

Our program is free to access, with additional features available through affordable membership. All proceeds go toward expanding our content and supporting families and educators who work with special needs learners.

Join us in creating a more inclusive world—one child, one smile, one step at a time.
