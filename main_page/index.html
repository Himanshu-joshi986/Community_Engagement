Get Started

At Amchi Shala, children have fun while they learn.

Amchi Shala was founded to support children with learning difficulties by offering a platform with untimed, multisensory interactive games that allow children to see, hear, and touch as they learn.

Amchi Shala was developed in classrooms with the help of teachers and educators, and is freely available to help children learn. It includes basic reading and math for preschool to primary levels. The program focuses on exploration, play, and positive encouragement—helping children become confident and curious learners. With content that feels like play, Amchi Shala provides a learning experience that’s an engaging alternative to typical screen time.

Amchi Shala’s free membership program offers even more learning tools like animated songs, math games, and extra reading activities. Your support helps us create new content like books, educational games, and videos that help children with different needs learn in joyful and meaningful ways.

Amchi Shala is an initiative by Amchi Shala Foundation, a nonprofit organization dedicated to making learning accessible, inclusive, and joyful for children with mental and developmental challenges. All content © 2025 Amchi Shala Foundation. All rights reserved.
